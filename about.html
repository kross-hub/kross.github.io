HEY, KAYLA HERE.

I am a UX/UI design professional with a background in sales, marketing, and business development and a passion for creative problem-solving. I use my work history to understand problems, explore motivations and craft solutions based on empathy and understanding.

I always had a passion for helping people figure out how to do things and solve problems in an intuitive way. With this in mind, I pursued a degree in Psychology with the hopes of really learning how people's minds work and in what ways I could make their lives easier. Ultimately, this led to a multitude of experience in research and analytics to figure out solutions to people's problems.

Fast forward a few years and I dove headfirst into a career in direct sales and marketing. Here I learned how to be faced with clients' problems and carefully weave a solution for them through empathy as well as the research and critical thinking which had been embedded in me through school.  I quickly realized I yearned to create! This led me down the path to learning to code and design with the intention of joining my skills to make a difference creating intuitive solutions for people. I am a designer who loves to join my fascination with how people's brains work with my passion to create.
